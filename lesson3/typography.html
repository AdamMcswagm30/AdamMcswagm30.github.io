Typography

Righteous:

This font will be use for the titles and the larger words appearing on my site. I chose this font because it looks nice, it is a little bolder than other fonts, and it is still very easy to read.

Lato:

Lato is the font that I chose for my normal words and paragraphs. It is a legible font that is not too out there or fancy, so that is why I chose to have this font be the paragraph font for my site.
