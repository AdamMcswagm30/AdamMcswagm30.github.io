Color Scheme

Primary:

#4381C1

This color will be the main color for the page. The primary color will probable be implemented as the background of the whole page, with a calming blue that reminds me of a gentle rainfall.

Secondary:

#BDADEA

My secondary color will be used for a lot of the backgrounds of titles and possibly the header and footer of the page to bring in a nice theme of more pastel looking colors rather than bright and vibrant ones.

Accent 1:

#404E5C

This accent will be used mostle for the words and titles on the page. Instead of having a boring black as the text color, I will use this as the color of the titles and maybe even for text or text boxes on my page.

Accent 2:

#FFCE22

My final accent will be for things that I really want to stand out seeing as though it is a brighter color that stands out in my color pallete. This might include important things on the page and will most likely be used in the hover effect for my navigations.
