Target Audience

Audience:

The targeted audience for my site will be people in need of inromation on the go. My site should be easy and quick enough to open up so that people that are very busy can check the weather without wasting too much of their precious time.

Personas:

Kenneth Brewer

Kenneth is a young company executive who lives a fast pace businessman's life. He is constantly on the double getting large projects done for his company and driving all over Idaho on important business meetings. He needs a weather app that shows the weather quickly and effeciently so that he can plan for his next trips in an effecient manner.

Jennifer Newton

Jennifer is a single mother of two who is constantly rushing to fulfill her children's needs. Her days are extremely busy trying to take care of both of her kids and she would really like to have a weather app that is quick and easy to use so that she can get the information she needs quickly.
